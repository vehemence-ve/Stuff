About Vehemence

Vehemence is the Sin of Wrath — ruler of the Wrathful Cities, commander of fire and fury, and patron of all things molten and loud. He rules Hell’s fiercest domain with an iron will and a lava crown.

His Family

His son, Kevin, is the heir to the Wrathful throne — a 16-year-old who would rather explore mortal fast-food culture than oversee demon legions. Vehemence may command armies, but even he can’t command a teenager.

Their Roles

Vehemence governs Wrath — the essence of raw, unrelenting emotion. His job is to maintain balance between destruction and discipline. Kevin serves as his apprentice, learning the fiery ways of rulership (when he’s not distracted by milkshakes).

Fun Facts

  • Vehemence once set an ocean on fire — for “aesthetic reasons.”
  • Kevin introduced Hell to Wi-Fi. It’s terrible, but it exists.
  • Vehemence hates cold weather — or as he calls it, “nature’s betrayal.”
  • Despite his title, he secretly enjoys mortal comedy shows.
